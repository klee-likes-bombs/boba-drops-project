FUN FACT OF THE DAY

The TARDIS wasn’t always supposed to look like a police box!

Originally, the TARDIS (Time And Relative Dimension In Space) was designed to change its appearance to blend in with its surroundings.

But after the first episode, the chameleon circuit “broke,” leaving it stuck as a 1960s British police box — a happy accident that became one of the show’s most iconic visuals.
